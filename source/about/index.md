I am a passionate and dedicated cybersecurity professional with a strong blend of offensive and defensive expertise.  
I graduated in 2021 and initially worked in a non-IT role, but cybersecurity has always been my long-term ambition.  
My interest began during my college years, where I envisioned joining an IT security team, with an ultimate goal of  
becoming a Chief Information Security Officer (CISO).

Since December, I have fully committed to transitioning into cybersecurity. I started my journey on **Hack The Box (HTB)**  
and actively practice **Capture The Flag (CTF)** challenges to strengthen my skills.  

I am particularly fascinated by **defensive security**, especially:
- Malware Analysis   
- Digital Forensics & Incident Response (DFIR)
- Threat Intelligence

I enjoy understanding the “behind-the-scenes” of cyberattacks and following an attacker’s trail — a key driving force  
in my development within the cyber defense domain.

## 🛠️ Professional Developments & Projects

### **PE Header Reconstruction**
Researching techniques to manually rebuild corrupted or deleted Windows Portable Executable (PE) headers —  
even when malware overwrites or randomizes header values.

### **PySecPolicy**
A Python forensic tool designed to parse and extract **User Rights Assignments (privileges)**  
from the Windows **SECURITY** hive for security auditing and DFIR investigations.

### **Regalyzer**
Developing a script-based toolkit for forensic analysis of Windows registry hives.  
Automates extraction of key artifacts to support digital forensics workflows.
